DIBOP

Dealer Integration Backbone & Orchestration Platform

Take ownership of how your business runs

What DIBOP Is

DIBOP is a dealer-owned execution layer that sits above OEMs, DMS, CRM, and enterprise systems.

Instead of business-critical processes being scattered across vendors, customisations, and manual workarounds, DIBOP gives dealerships a single place to define how key decisions and workflows should operate — and have systems execute them consistently.

What Problem It Solves

In most dealerships, the logic that governs pricing, approvals, compliance, and handoffs lives partly in vendor software and partly in people's heads.

This makes change slow, auditability difficult, and vendor dependency unavoidable.

DIBOP addresses this by separating business decision-making from system execution, allowing dealerships to evolve their operations without being constrained by vendor roadmaps.

How It's Used

DIBOP is typically introduced through mandatory OEM integrations, such as Mercedes-Benz OneAPI, where orchestration and governance are already required.

From there, dealerships can choose — at their own pace — whether to extend the same approach to other systems and processes.

What It Is Not

❌

Not a DMS, CRM, or ERP Replacement

DIBOP does not replace your existing systems of record

❌

Not a System Duplication

DIBOP does not duplicate systems of record

❌

Not a Fixed Platform

DIBOP does not impose a fixed platform roadmap

It exists to give the business control over how systems are used — not to replace them.
